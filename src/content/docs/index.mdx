## 🎯 About Schrödinger Hat

Schrödinger Hat is a non-profit organization dedicated to making open source truly open. We believe in:

- **Community First**: Building inclusive, welcoming communities
- **Open Knowledge**: Sharing knowledge freely and openly
- **Innovation**: Pushing the boundaries of what's possible
- **Education**: Empowering people through technology

### Our Impact

- **20k+ Reach**: Unique presences at our events, online and in-person
- **100+ Speakers**: Industry leaders that gave talks from our events stage
- **49+ Hours**: Videos, podcasts, and livestreams from our events
- **20+ Contributors**: People actively contributing to our projects
